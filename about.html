B.M.R Law Offices is a
professionally managed Law Firm
with a team of remarkably talented legal associates
based in Hyderabad.

We'd rather be honest than impressive

Our objective is to look for simple, efficient, and uncompromising solutions to what may be perceived as complicated problems.

Our practice areas include (but not limited to) Dispute Resolution, Arbitration, Litigation, Civil & Criminal Matters, Family and Personal, Corporate & Commercial, Information Technology, Agency & Franchise, Employment & Service Matters, Consumer Protection Laws, Cyber Crime, Transfer of Technology, Construction & Infrastructure, Contract support, Intellectual Property, Mergers & Acquisitions, Joint Ventures etc. We represent clients ranging from multi-national corporations to small businesses, and individuals.

Our dedicated team takes pleasure in understanding client specific issues, priorities, concerns and analyze them in the circumstances of commercial realities. We have the ability to visualize, plan and help our clients to achieve the best result.

To serve the regulatory needs of our clients, we work with a number of consultants who have held Senior Government positions in various departments. We support numerous public Interest litigations of local importance by dedicating time, and financial resources to improve our communities and help those in need.

We strictly maintain the highest level of professional ethics with an excellent, responsive and timely legal service to our clients. We distinguish our self by being Focused, Accessible, and Responsive. B.M.R Law Offices believes in making legal services available to all who need them, and our lawyers are involved in pro bono work on behalf of various individuals and organizations.
